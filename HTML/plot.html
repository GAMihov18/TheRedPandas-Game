PLOT

Bella, Robert, Ursule and Harry met a wizzard who cast a spell on them. He first cloned them three times, so now there are four of each person. They are all now pawns in his maze. Only the real ones can escape and reach the marked spaces in the middle. Once all four of them reach the spots, the curse will be broken. The clones will all disappear and Bella, Robert, Ursule and Harry will finally be able to go back to their homes.
